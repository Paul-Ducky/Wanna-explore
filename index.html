Wanna Explore

Came to exist out of the vibrant passion we each share for traveling to remote places where we could experience nature in her most pure, divert and attractive state. We are five longtime world explorers who each have their own expertise of adventure and love to share these with anybody who is in need for a thrill or rather seeks to have some relax time. We offer tailor made experiences for all of you who seek to go out there. With a wide variety of options for all kinds of traveling.

Exploring majestic mountains in winter and hiking through lovely green countrysides in spring. Or would you rather be visiting lesser known regions on sunny summer islands and taking the most breathtaking pictures at the perfect hideout when fall falls over the trees. At your pace, with your wishes. We will be there upfront or in the background!
